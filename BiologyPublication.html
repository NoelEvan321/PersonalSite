Gli Expression in Cancer

Under the guidance of Dr. James Kim, I worked to better understand the molecular mechanisms involved in the Hedgehog Signaling Pathway. We sought to gain insight into the roles of the Sonic, Indian, and Desert Hedgehog proteins in the tumor micro-environment.
